Education

I am currently enrolled in edX Boot Camps partnering with The Ohio State University to study Fullstack Web Development. The program that I am in is a 24 week intensive course that teaches students the fundamentals of web development, including the following.

  • How to build front-end websites from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • How to create full-stack single-page web applications using RESTful API routes and AJAX methods
  • How to implement structured and unstructured databases to convert static websites into dynamic websites that persist data
  • The fundamentals of source control, issue tracking, and functional feedback
  • Working in teams and using project management skills

Passion

My passion for programming began during my High School career when I decided to enroll in AP Computer Science Principles my senior year. In that class, I learned the basics of how the internet and computers worked and the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. From there, I gained the majority of my knowledge of programming when I studied Computer Science at Kent State University from 2019 to 2021. After coming to the decision to hault my education due to personal reasons, I started working as a server at a small Italian Restaurant. However, as time grew, I knew I wanted to get back into programming and reignite my passion for coding. This is when I came across The Ohio State's Coding Boot Camp with edX. After doing my research on the curriculum of the 24 week intensive course, I knew it would be the perfect opportunity for me to jump back into a future filled with endless potential in the booming age of technology. I am incredibly excited to go forth with my decision to continue my education and the opportunities that will arise from it.
